The Best Movies of Goutham

My top 3 movies of all-time

Chanthupottu

Chandupottu is a 2005 Malayalam film that follows the story of a man with effeminate mannerisms, exploring themes of identity, societal norms, and acceptance with a mix of drama and comedy.

Nanpakal nerathu mayakkam

​Nanpakal Nerathu Mayakkam is a 2023 Malayalam-Tamil bilingual film directed by Lijo Jose Pellissery, featuring Mammootty in the lead role.

Empuraa

​L2: Empuraan is a 2025 Malayalam action thriller directed by Prithviraj Sukumaran, serving as a sequel to the 2019 film Lucifer
